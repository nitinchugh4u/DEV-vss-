Ninja is a Famous Punjabi Singer as well as Actor

The first Song that Sidhu moosewala wrote was sung by the Ninja.The Song name Was License

He Started his Singing Journey from Punjabi Boliyan and Bhangra (Punjabi Dialects)

Now,He is very popular for his songs like Aadat, Dil , Roi na , chor , oh kyu ni jaan sake and many more

Ninja is a Versatile Singer,He can Sing Sad, Romantic and more more type of songs like Mafia style
He is my favourate singer as well

Ninja hailing from Ludhiana, Punjab. In an interview, Ninja stated that he faced many difficulties during his struggling period. He received his musical training from Ustad Harvinder Bittu Ji in Ludhiana. In his college days, Ninja held a part-time job in the telecom industry.
